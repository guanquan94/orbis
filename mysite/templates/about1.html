About Us

This site is created by Zhi Yi and Guan Quan. The main usage of this website would be to view when the next episode of your favourite movie is about to come out!

To utilize this site, follow the details below:

Reviews

Step 1:

1. Click on the Reviews that interests you.

Step 2:

2. If you have watched the TV show before, you can add it to your "Watched List"

Step 3:

Under [1], you can input your own ratings.

Under [2], you can add this TV show to your calendar.

Under [3], you can share it on your social media platforms with your friends.

Step 4:

Under [1], you can input your Start Date and Time in the format required.

Under [2], you can input your End Date and Time in the format required. Tip: End Date should be the same day.

Under [3], the title of the TV show will be automatically added according to the title.

Under [4], you can input your own description.

Under [5], you can ignore the remaining field and click save.

Step 5:

You can continue to make further edits or click on Scheduler to continue on!

However, you will be required to create a calendar first. Please continue on through the Scheduler.

Scheduler

Step 1:

You will be prompted to create a Calendar. Hence click on the "Create Calendar" button and input your desired name!

However, you will be required to create a calendar first. Please continue on through the Scheduler.

Step 2:

After completing Step 1, your Calendar will be ready!

Note: You can only create ONE Calendar. Trying to create more than ONE will result in a message popout stating "You cannot create two calendars."

Step 3:

After that, your TV show will be reflected on your Calendar.

Friend and Watch List

Step 1:

You can locate your Watch List under your Profile on the Navigation Bar.

Step 2:

Under the same tab, you can select Friend List and you will be redirected to this page.

Here, you can find your friend's username and add them to your Friend List.

Step 3:

Under the Friend Request List, you can see the Friend Request that you have sent out and received!

You can also view your received Friend Request and add them to your Friend List!

Step 4:

Under the Friend List, you can see your list of friends that have accepted your Friend Request.

You can then proceed to view what TV shows have they watched through "View Watched List".

Step 5:

Here, you can see their watched list for the TV shows.

You can prevent yourself from accidentally providing spoilers!

The End. Brought to you by Project Orbis!
